Tap any 🎤 button and speak instead of type.

FEATURES AT A GLANCE

📅
Content Calendar
Plan videos by date — content type, book, hashtags, notes. Check off when posted.
📖
Scorecard
Log finished books with ratings, genre, Mind Scale, platform badges (Kindle · Audible · Hoopla · Libby · Spotify · Physical), and review notes.
📚
TBR Pile
Save books you want to read. Tap "Generate Script" on any book for a ready-made TikTok TBR script.
✍️
Caption Generator
Choose content type, fill in the prompts, get a ready-to-paste TikTok caption.
📦
Book Haul Captions
Pick your source (Barnes & Noble, Library, Amazon, etc.) and get a caption written for that specific haul.
🏷️
Hashtag Sets
Curated hashtags by content type — tap to copy, paste into TikTok.
📈
Follower Tracker
Log your count weekly and watch your growth chart build over time.
🎯
Engagement Tracker
Log views, likes, comments, shares, and saves per video to see what content actually performs.
📬
ARC & Collab Log
Track every ARC request and brand pitch — status, deadline, and notes all in one place.
💡
Idea Generator
Tap for a random content idea when you're stuck. Keep tapping until something sparks.
🏆
Wins Journal
Log every milestone, viral video, and personal win. Read it back when you're feeling burnt out.
🎡
Spin the Wheel
Pulls from your TBR automatically. Tap SPIN and commit to whatever it lands on — great content to film live.
📊
Analytics
Monthly stats — books read, growth, top video, genre mix. Screenshot for your monthly wrap-up video.
💬
Quotes
Save favorite lines as you read. Use as text overlays or to open a review video.
✏️
Quick Notes
Scratch pad — auto-saves as you type. Mic appends spoken text without overwriting what's there.

BACKUP & SETTINGS

💾 BACKUP REMINDER

Your data saves on your device, not the cloud. Go to Backup & Sync → Backup My Data once a week. To move to a new device: back up → send the file → restore.

⚙️ SETTINGS

Tap the gear icon anytime to update your name, hashtag, or color theme — Gold · Crimson · Amethyst · Teal · Rose · Silver · Emerald.
