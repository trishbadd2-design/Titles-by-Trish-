📋 REVIEW VIDEO BLUEPRINT
▼
Hit these 5 things in every review — in this order:
① HOOK — first 3 seconds
One line that makes them stop scrolling. Lead with the emotion, not the plot. "This book made me feel genuinely unsafe and I loved every second of it."
② SETUP — 10 seconds
Title + author + genre + one-sentence premise. Keep it punchy. No synopsis recapping — just the vibe.
③ THE MOMENT — 15 seconds
One specific scene, line, or twist that made you react (no spoilers). This is what drives saves and shares — people want to know what got you.
④ MIND SCALE RATING — 5 seconds
Show your rating card. Pause on it. Let the number land. "Official TitlesByTrish rating: Completely Unhinged."
⑤ CTA — last 5 seconds
Ask ONE question. "Have you read this? What's your Mind Scale?" or "What genre do you want me to wreck next?" — drives comments.
GENRE-SPECIFIC TIPS
Psych Thriller: Lead with the unreliable narrator or the moment you stopped trusting the protagonist.
Horror: Describe the physical feeling — did you feel sick? Scared? Unsafe? That reaction IS the content.
True Crime: Open with one fact that floors the viewer. Everything else is context.
Dark Fantasy: The world-building hook — one sentence about the setting that sounds like nothing else.
Historical Fiction: The era + the dark twist. "1800s England + secret society + body count."
BOOK HAUL FORMULA
Hook: show all books, don't reveal titles yet.
For each book: cover reveal → one-sentence premise → why you got it.
End with: "which one should I read first?" — best engagement driver for hauls.
