──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  🎤  MIC BUTTON TIP (works everywhere)
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Tap any 🎤 button and speak instead of type.
  On title fields, say "Book Title by Author Name" and it
  fills BOTH fields at once automatically.


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  WHAT EACH SECTION DOES
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  📅  Content Calendar
      Plan your videos by date. Pick content type, book,
      hashtag set, and notes. Check off when posted.

  📖  Scorecard
      Log every book you finish — ratings, genre, Mind Scale,
      platform badges (Kindle/Audible/Hoopla/Libby/Spotify/
      Physical), and review notes. Your reading history lives here.

  📚  TBR Pile
      Add books you want to read. Tap "Generate Script" on any
      book for a ready-made TikTok TBR script.

  ✍️  Caption Generator
      Pick a content type, fill in the prompts, get a
      ready-to-paste TikTok caption.

  📦  Book Haul Captions
      Choose your source (Barnes & Noble, Library, Amazon, etc.)
      and get a caption written for that specific haul.

  🏷️  Hashtag Sets
      Curated hashtag sets by content type — tap to copy,
      paste into TikTok.

  📈  Follower Tracker
      Log your count weekly. Watch your growth chart build.

  🎯  Engagement Tracker
      Log views, likes, comments, shares, and saves per video
      to see what content actually performs.

  📬  ARC & Collab Log
      Track every ARC request and brand pitch — status,
      deadline, and notes all in one place.

  💡  Idea Generator
      Tap to get a random content idea when you're stuck.

  🏆  Wins Journal
      Log every milestone, viral video, and personal win.
      Read it back when you're feeling burnt out.

  🎡  Spin the Wheel
      Pulls from your TBR automatically. Tap SPIN and commit
      to whatever it lands on. Great content to film live.

  📊  Analytics
      Monthly stats — books read, growth, top video, genre mix.
      Screenshot it for your monthly wrap-up video.

  💬  Quotes
      Save favorite lines as you read. Use as text overlays
      or to open a review video.

  ✏️  Quick Notes
      Scratch pad — auto-saves as you type. Use the 🎤 mic
      to speak notes (appends, doesn't overwrite).


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  💾  BACKUP REMINDER
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Your data saves on your device, not the cloud.
  Go to Backup & Sync → "Backup My Data" once a week.
  To move to a new device: back up → send file → restore.


──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ⚙️  SETTINGS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Tap the gear icon anytime to change your name, hashtag,
  or color theme (Gold · Crimson · Amethyst · Teal ·
  Rose · Silver · Emerald).
