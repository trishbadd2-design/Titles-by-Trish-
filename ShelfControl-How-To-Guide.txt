  1. CONTENT CALENDAR
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The Content Calendar is your planning hub — every video idea
from now through the end of the year, all in one place.

HOW TO ADD A VIDEO PLAN
  1. Tap "Content Calendar" from the home screen
  2. Select the Date you want to post
  3. Choose a Content Type from the dropdown:
       Book Review · Book Haul · Reading Vlog · TBR/Wrap Up
       Discussion · Recommendation · Story Time · Trend
  4. Enter the Book Title (if it's book-specific)
  5. Add your Hashtag Set (tap to auto-fill from your saved sets)
  6. Add any Notes — your hook idea, talking points, or reminder
  7. Tap "Add to Calendar"

HOW TO USE IT
  · Scroll through the calendar to see your plan at a glance
  · Tap any entry to expand and see the full details
  · Check off entries as "posted" so you always know where you are
  · Use it to make sure you're posting a variety of content types
    (not five reviews in a row!)

PRO TIP: Plan out one month at a time every Sunday. Pick your
content types first, then fill in which books for each slot.

  2. SCORECARD — YOUR BOOK LOG
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The Scorecard is where you log every book you finish. It tracks
more than just a star rating — it gives you real data about
your reading and what your audience will love.

HOW TO LOG A BOOK
  1. Tap "Scorecard" from the home screen
  2. Enter the Book Title and Author
     (or tap the 🎤 mic and say "Book Title by Author Name" —
     it fills BOTH fields automatically!)
  3. Add the Date Started and Date Finished
  4. Choose your Genre
  5. Rate each category on a scale of 1–5:
       ✦ Plot         ✦ Characters    ✦ Writing Style
       ✦ Pacing       ✦ Ending        ✦ Emotional Hit
  6. Overall Rating auto-calculates from your scores
  7. Set the Mind Scale — how heavy was this book mentally?
       Light Read · Mild · Moderate · Heavy · Devastating
  8. Select your platform badges (where you read it):
       📱 Kindle  🎧 Audible  🎵 Spotify  📗 Hoopla  📕 Libby  📖 Physical
  9. Add your Review Notes — your honest thoughts
  10. Tap "✦ Save to Scorecard"

WHAT TO DO WITH IT
  · Look back before you film a review — all your notes are there
  · Sort by rating to see your top reads of the year
  · Check your genre mix — are you in a reading rut?
  · Use the Mind Scale to recommend books to your audience
    ("if you want something light and fun, THIS one" /
     "this one absolutely destroyed me in the best way")

PRO TIP: Log the book immediately when you finish — while your
feelings are still fresh. You don't have to film right away,
but capture those first reactions now.

  3. TBR PILE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Your TBR (To Be Read) list lives here. Add books as you hear
about them so you never forget a recommendation again.

HOW TO ADD A BOOK
  1. Tap "TBR Pile" from the home screen
  2. Enter the Book Title and Author
     (or tap 🎤 and say "Book Title by Author Name")
  3. Set your Interest Level: ★ through ★★★★★
  4. Add where you heard about it (TikTok rec, friend, etc.)
  5. Select platform badges for where you plan to read it
  6. Tap "✦ Add to TBR"

FEATURES
  · Sort by interest level — your most-anticipated books first
  · Tap "Generate Video Script" on any book for a ready-made
    TikTok-style TBR script you can read on camera
  · Move a book to your Scorecard when you start reading it
  · Remove books you've decided to skip without guilt

AUTO-GENERATED TBR SCRIPT EXAMPLE:
  "Okay, adding this one to my TBR and I cannot wait —
   [Book Title] by [Author]. I heard about this from [source]
   and the premise sounds absolutely [your notes]. This is
   giving me [vibe] energy and I need it NOW."

PRO TIP: Use your TBR as content! Film a "books on my TBR"
video straight from this list — your most-anticipated books
sorted and ready to talk about.

  4. CAPTION GENERATOR
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Never stare at a blank caption box again. The Caption Generator
has ready-made captions for every type of BookTok video.

HOW TO USE IT
  1. Tap "Caption Generator" from the home screen
  2. Choose your Content Type:
       Book Review · Books I Recommend · Reading Vlog
       Discussion · Book Haul · Wrap Up · Reading Challenge
  3. Fill in the prompts (book title, your rating, one-word
     description, etc.)
  4. Tap "Generate Caption"
  5. Copy and paste it straight into TikTok

CAPTION TYPES INCLUDED
  · Book Review  — for your detailed review videos
  · Recommendation  — for "you need to read this" videos
  · Reading Vlog  — for reading-with-me / weekly vlog content
  · Discussion  — for hot takes and bookish debates
  · Book Haul  — for showing off new books
  · Monthly Wrap Up  — for your end-of-month recap videos
  · Reading Challenge  — for challenge update videos

PRO TIP: Customize the generated caption before posting —
add your own personality, specific quotes from the book,
or a personal story. The generator gives you the structure;
you make it yours.

  5. BOOK HAUL CAPTIONS
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Book haul captions — already written and tagged by where you
bought the books.

HOW TO USE IT
  1. Tap "Book Haul" from the home screen
  2. Select your source:
       Barnes & Noble · Book Outlet · Amazon · Library
       Thrift Store · Online Indie · TBR Subscription Box
  3. Add the books you're showing (title + author)
  4. Tap "Generate Haul Caption"
  5. Copy the caption — it includes source-specific wording
     and is ready to paste into TikTok

EACH SOURCE HAS ITS OWN VIBE
  Barnes & Noble  — "I can never leave without spending $$$"
  Book Outlet     — "The deals were too good, I had no choice"
  Library         — "Free books? Say less. #LibraryTok"
  Thrift Store    — "Thrift finds are hitting different lately"

PRO TIP: These captions work for Instagram Reels and YouTube
Shorts too — not just TikTok.

  6. HASHTAG SETS
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Curated hashtag sets for every type of BookTok content —
so you're never copy-pasting from your notes app again.

HOW TO USE IT
  1. Tap "Hashtags" from the home screen
  2. Choose a set by content type:
       General BookTok · Book Review · Book Haul
       TBR & Wrap Up · Reading Vlog · Discussion
       Romance · Fantasy · Thriller · Dark/Spicy · Cozy
  3. Tap the set to expand and copy all hashtags
  4. Paste directly into your TikTok caption

TIPS FOR HASHTAGS
  · Use a mix of large tags (#BookTok) and niche tags
    (#DarkRomance, #ThrillerTok) — the niche ones get you
    to the right audience faster
  · Swap in your personal hashtag so followers can find all
    your content in one place
  · Don't use the same 30 tags every single video — rotate
    between the sets to stay fresh

PRO TIP: Your personal hashtag is already set up from your
onboarding. Look for it automatically added to every set.

  7. FOLLOWER TRACKER
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Log your follower count regularly and watch your growth over
time — because the numbers matter and you deserve to celebrate.

HOW TO USE IT
  1. Tap "Followers" from the home screen
  2. Tap "Log Today's Count"
  3. Enter your current follower count
  4. Add a note if something happened (viral video, collab, etc.)
  5. Tap "Save"

WHAT YOU'LL SEE
  · Your growth chart — visually shows your upward trend
  · How many followers you've gained this week / month
  · Your fastest growth days (so you know what content works!)
  · Milestone badges when you hit major counts

MILESTONES TO LOG
  500  ·  1K  ·  5K  ·  10K  ·  25K  ·  50K  ·  100K

PRO TIP: Log every Sunday. That one weekly check-in gives you
all the data you need — weekly growth, monthly trends, and
which weeks your content hit hardest.


══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  8. ENGAGEMENT TRACKER
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Track how individual videos perform so you can double down on
what works and stop wasting time on what doesn't.

HOW TO LOG A VIDEO
  1. Tap "Engagement" from the home screen
  2. Tap "Add Video"
  3. Enter the video title / description
  4. Enter the stats:
       Views · Likes · Comments · Shares · Saves
  5. Select the Content Type (Review, Haul, Vlog, etc.)
  6. Add any notes about why you think it performed that way
  7. Tap "Save"

WHAT TO LOOK FOR
  · Which content types get the most saves?
     (Saves = TikTok loves it — push more of that)
  · Which videos got shared the most?
     (Shares = your audience loves it enough to send to a friend)
  · Do certain book genres perform better than others?
  · Do your longer videos outperform short ones, or vice versa?

PRO TIP: You don't need to track every video. Pick your top
3–5 videos from the week and log those. Patterns show up fast.

  9. ARC & COLLAB LOG
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Track every ARC (Advance Review Copy) request and brand collab
pitch — so nothing falls through the cracks.

HOW TO LOG AN ARC
  1. Tap "ARC & Collab" from the home screen
  2. Tap "Add ARC"
  3. Enter:
       Book Title · Author / Publisher · Request Date
       Review Deadline · Status (Requested / Approved / Posted)
       Notes (any specific requirements from the publisher)
  4. Tap "Save"

HOW TO LOG A COLLAB PITCH
  1. Tap "Add Collab"
  2. Enter:
       Brand Name · Contact Info · Pitch Date
       Offer Details · Response Status · Deadline
  3. Tap "Save"

STATUS OPTIONS
  · Requested — waiting to hear back
  · Approved — you've been accepted!
  · In Progress — reading / filming
  · Posted — review is live
  · Declined — they passed (keep pitching!)
  · Expired — missed the deadline (it happens — note it and move on)

PRO TIP: Set your review deadline two weeks BEFORE the book's
release date. That gives you time to film, edit, and post
without scrambling at the last minute.

  12. SPIN THE WHEEL
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Can't decide what to read next? Let the wheel decide.

HOW TO USE IT
  1. Tap "Spin the Wheel" from the home screen
  2. The wheel is automatically filled with books from your TBR
  3. Tap "SPIN"
  4. Whatever it lands on — that's your next read. No takebacks.

CUSTOMIZING THE WHEEL
  · The wheel pulls from your TBR automatically
  · You can add or remove books from your TBR to change
    what appears on the wheel
  · Want a specific vibe? Filter your TBR by genre first,
    then spin

PRO TIP: This makes incredible content. Film yourself spinning
the wheel and let your audience watch in real time. They love
the anticipation — and they'll be invested in the book once
they see you commit to it.

  16. VOICE / MIC INPUT
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Many fields throughout the app have a 🎤 mic button.
Tap it and speak instead of type — the app listens and
fills the field automatically.

WHERE MIC BUTTONS APPEAR
  · Scorecard: Book Title, Author
  · TBR Pile: Book Title, Author
  · Wins Journal: Win title field
  · Quotes: Quote text, Book title
  · Quick Notes: Main notes area

THE MAGIC "TITLE BY AUTHOR" TRICK
  On Title fields, you can say the WHOLE thing at once:

  Say: "The Dead and the Dark by Courtney Gould"

  The app automatically fills:
    Title → "The Dead and the Dark"
    Author → "Courtney Gould"

  You don't have to tap two separate mic buttons.

HOW TO USE THE MIC
  1. Tap the 🎤 button — it turns red and pulses
  2. Speak clearly
  3. When you stop speaking, the field fills automatically
  4. Tap 🎤 again at any time to stop listening

TROUBLESHOOTING
  · "Mic access denied" — Go to your browser/app settings
    and allow microphone access for this site
  · Not working at all — Try Chrome on Android or Safari on
    iPhone. Some older browsers don't support voice input.
  · Speaking but nothing appears — Speak a little slower and
    make sure you have a good connection

  17. BACKUP & RESTORE
══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Your app saves everything to your device. Back up regularly
so you never lose your data.

HOW TO BACK UP
  1. From the home screen, tap "Backup & Sync"
     (or tap the ⚙️ gear icon → Backup)
  2. Tap "Backup My Data"
  3. A file downloads to your device (it's tiny — just text)
  4. Keep this file somewhere safe: iCloud, Google Drive,
     your email, or AirDrop it to another device

HOW TO RESTORE
  1. Open your app
  2. Go to Backup & Sync → "Restore Backup"
  3. Select the backup file you saved
  4. Done — all your data is back

MOVING TO A NEW DEVICE
  1. Back up on your old device (steps above)
  2. Send the file to your new device via AirDrop, email,
     or save it to cloud storage
  3. Open the app on your new device (use the same link)
  4. Restore the backup file

BACK UP HOW OFTEN?
  Once a week is a great habit — same as saving a document.
  Back up before clearing your browser cache or switching
  devices, no matter what.

  QUICK TIPS FOR MAKING THE MOST OF SHELFCONTROL

✦ LOG AS YOU GO — The app works best when you use it daily.
  Spend 2 minutes logging a book or video right after it happens.

  your Analytics page. It's perfect monthly wrap-up content.

✦ WINS JOURNAL MATTERS — Log even the small stuff. Reading
  your wins back is the best cure for creator burnout.

✦ LET THE WHEEL DECIDE — When you can't pick a next read and
  you need content, spin the wheel on camera. Every time.
